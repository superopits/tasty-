Narendra Modi

  • Born in Vadnagar, Gujarat, on September 17, 1950
  • Joined the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) at age 8
  • Became the Bharatiya Janata Party's (BJP) national secretary in 1995
  • Served as Gujarat's Chief Minister from 2001 to 2014
  • Implemented the "Vibrant Gujarat" initiative to attract investments
  • Known for his "Make in India" and "Swachh Bharat" campaigns
  • Played a key role in implementing the Unified Payments Interface (UPI)
  • Instrumental in the abrogation of Article 370, granting special status to Jammu and Kashmir
  • First Indian Prime Minister to be born after Independence
  • Speaks fluent Hindi, Gujarati, and English

Rahul Gandhi

  • Born in New Delhi, India, on June 19, 1970
  • Grandson of Jawaharlal Nehru and Indira Gandhi
  • Served as the Congress Party's Vice President from 2013 to 2019
  • Elected as the Lok Sabha MP from Amethi in 2004
  • Advocates for farmers' rights and rural development
  • Supports the "Make in India" initiative
  • Has a focus on empowering youth and promoting education
  • Visited the United States in 2017 to address the University of California, Berkeley
  • Has a reputation for being a humble and approachable leader
  • Speaks fluent Hindi, English, and some Italian

Arvind Kejriwal

  • Born in Hisar, Haryana, on August 16, 1968
  • Graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur
  • Served as a Joint Commissioner in the Income Tax Department
  • Founded the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) in 2012
  • Became the Delhi Chief Minister in 2013 and again in 2015
  • Implemented the "Odd-Even" scheme to reduce pollution
  • Focused on improving education and healthcare in Delhi
  • Advocates for transparency and accountability in governance
  • Has a reputation for being a passionate and committed leader
  • Speaks fluent Hindi, English, and some Punjabi
